<p><p>The Fiber Optic MEMS Switches market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches use a single light path to transmit signals over long distances, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed data applications. In contrast, multimode switches operate with multiple light paths, allowing for shorter-distance transmissions and applications in data centers and local area networks. Both types enhance data routing efficiency and performance in optical communication systems.</p></p>
