<p><p>The electrocardiogram (ECG) paper market is categorized primarily into single roll and Z-fold types. Single roll ECG paper consists of a continuous sheet wound on a roll, facilitating quick and seamless printing during diagnostics. In contrast, Z-fold ECG paper is pre-folded in a zigzag pattern, allowing for easy handling and storage while providing an organized presentation of the recorded data. Both formats cater to different user preferences and equipment requirements in medical environments, enhancing the efficiency of cardiac monitoring.</p></p>
