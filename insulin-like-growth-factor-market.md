<p><p>The Insulin-Like Growth Factor (IGF) market consists of various types, including Mechano Growth Factor (MGF), Somatomedin C, and IGF-1. MGF is a variant associated with muscle repair and growth, typically produced in response to physical stress. Somatomedin C, primarily circulating in the bloodstream, mediates growth hormone effects on tissues. IGF-1, the most studied form, influences cell growth and proliferation. Together, these factors play significant roles in growth, metabolism, and tissue regeneration, driving diverse applications in health and fitness industries.</p></p>
