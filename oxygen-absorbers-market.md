<p><p>The oxygen absorbers market comprises two main types: ferrous type and organic type. Ferrous type oxygen absorbers use iron powder to remove oxygen from sealed packages, effectively preventing spoilage and extending shelf life. They are suitable for various food products. Organic type oxygen absorbers, on the other hand, utilize natural materials such as enzymes or other biodegradable components. These are often preferred for organic and natural food products, aligning with eco-friendly consumer preferences while also inhibiting oxidation and microbial growth.</p></p>
