<p><p>Air core coils are inductors without a magnetic core, used in applications requiring minimal interference and high-frequency performance. In the market, they are categorized into circular and spiral types. Spiral air core coils feature a flat, coiled structure, providing compactness and precise inductance, suitable for compact electronic devices and sensors. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing performance and space efficiency.</p></p>
