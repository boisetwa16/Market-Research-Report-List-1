<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Washing System market includes three main types: Tunnel Type, Rollovers Type, and Touchless In-Bay systems. Tunnel Type systems use a conveyor belt to move vehicles through a series of cleaning stages, ideal for high-volume operations. Rollovers Type systems feature stationary vehicles with rotating brushes or spray arms moving around them, suitable for smaller spaces. Touchless In-Bay systems rely on high-pressure water and detergents without physical contact, offering a scratch-free wash for delicate vehicles. Each type caters to different needs, balancing efficiency, space, and vehicle care.</p></p>
