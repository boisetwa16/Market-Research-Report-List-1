Table frame vibration meters are designed for stability and accuracy on flat surfaces, suitable for laboratory use. Sponge vibration meters are portable and flexible, enabling measurement on varied surfaces. Each type serves specific needs in monitoring and analyzing vibrations across industries.</p></p>
