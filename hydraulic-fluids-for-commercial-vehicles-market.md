<p><p>Hydraulic fluids for commercial vehicles are categorized based on their flash points, which indicate their resistance to ignition under high temperatures. Fluids with a **140°C flash point** are suitable for standard applications with moderate heat exposure. Those with a **220°C flash point** offer enhanced safety and performance for heavier-duty operations. Fluids with a **300°C flash point** are ideal for extreme conditions, ensuring stability and fire resistance. The **Others** category includes specialized formulations tailored for unique operational requirements, such as biodegradable or synthetic options.</p></p>
