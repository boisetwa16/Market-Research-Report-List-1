<p><p>The automatic tissue paper dispenser market is primarily categorized into two types: surface-mounted and countertop-mounted dispensers. Surface-mounted dispensers are fixed to walls, saving valuable counter space and providing easy access in high-traffic areas, ideal for public restrooms or commercial settings. Conversely, countertop-mounted dispensers are placed on surfaces like countertops or tables, offering convenience for use in both residential and small business environments. Both types enhance hygiene and reduce tissue waste by dispensing controlled amounts of paper.</p></p>
