<p><p>The Teardrop Pallet Rack market consists of two main types: bolted and welded racks. Bolted type racks utilize detachable components that can be easily assembled and disassembled, offering flexibility for reconfiguration and customization. Conversely, welded type racks are constructed from permanently fused components, providing enhanced durability and stability, ideal for heavy loads. Both types cater to varying storage requirements in warehouses and distribution centers, balancing between ease of installation and structural integrity for efficient inventory management.</p></p>
