<p><strong>The Desktop Keyboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical keyboard</li><li>Ordinary keyboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop keyboard market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and ordinary keyboards. Mechanical keyboards feature individual mechanical switches for each key, offering tactile feedback and durability, making them preferred by gamers and typing enthusiasts. In contrast, ordinary keyboards, often membrane-based, use a rubber dome under the keys, providing softer feedback and a quieter experience, appealing to general users for everyday tasks. Each type serves distinct user preferences, influencing their design and functionality in the market.</p></p>
