<p><strong>The Snake Venom Antiserum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvalent</li><li>Monovalent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snake venom antiserum market is categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent antiserum. Polyvalent antiserum is designed to neutralize venom from multiple snake species, making it useful in diverse regions where different snakes are prevalent. In contrast, monovalent antiserum targets the venom of a single snake species, offering a more specific treatment option. The choice between these types depends on the geographical distribution of snake species and the nature of snakebite incidents in a given area.</p></p>
