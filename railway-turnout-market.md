<p><strong>The Railway Turnout Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Crossover</li><li>Double Crossover</li><li>Track Crossing</li><li>Slip Turnout</li><li>Lapped Turnouts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Railway Turnout Market includes various types designed for track flexibility and efficiency. **Single Crossover** allows trains to switch between two parallel tracks. **Double Crossover** enables simultaneous switching across two tracks. **Track Crossing** facilitates the intersection of two tracks at a right angle. **Slip Turnout** combines a crossover and turnout, allowing trains to switch or cross tracks seamlessly. **Lapped Turnouts** involve overlapping turnouts for compact layouts, optimizing space in complex rail networks. Each type serves specific operational needs, enhancing rail system functionality.</p></p>
