<p><p>The photography lighting equipment market encompasses two primary types: instantaneous lighting and continuous lighting. Instantaneous lighting involves flashes or strobes that emit brief bursts of light, ideal for freezing motion and providing high-intensity illumination for short durations. In contrast, continuous lighting systems offer a constant light source, allowing photographers to see shadows and highlights in real time, which is particularly useful for video and product photography. Each type serves distinct creative needs, catering to various photographic applications.</p></p>
