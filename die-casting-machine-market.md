<p><strong>The Die Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die casting machine market is primarily divided into two types: hot chamber and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber machines use a melting pot to inject molten metal directly into the mold, making them suitable for low-melting-point alloys and high production rates. In contrast, cold chamber machines require the molten metal to be poured into the chamber before injection, making them ideal for high-melting-point materials, offering versatility but typically lower production speeds. Both types cater to varied industrial applications.</p></p>
