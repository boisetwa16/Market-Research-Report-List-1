<p><ul><li>Fiberglass Posts</li><li>Carbon Fiber Posts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental fiber post market primarily comprises two types: fiberglass posts and carbon fiber posts. Fiberglass posts are made from a composite of glass fibers and resin, offering excellent aesthetics, flexibility, and bond strength with tooth structure. They are widely used for anterior and posterior restorations. On the other hand, carbon fiber posts have high strength and rigidity, making them suitable for supporting heavier loads in posterior teeth. Both types enhance the longevity and function of dental restorations.</p></p>
