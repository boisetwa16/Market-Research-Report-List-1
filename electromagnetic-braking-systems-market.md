<p><p>Electromagnetic braking systems encompass various types suited for diverse applications. Single face brakes are compact and efficient, providing direct engagement. Power off brakes activate when power is cut, ensuring safety and reliable stopping. Particle brakes utilize friction among particles to slow down motion effectively. Hysteresis power brakes function through magnetic fields to generate smooth deceleration. Multiple disk brakes combine several discs for enhanced stopping power, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications where high torque and reliable performance are essential.</p></p>
