<p><strong>The Distribution Voltage Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant Switching</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distribution voltage regulator market is primarily segmented into two types: ferroresonant and tap switching regulators. Ferroresonant regulators utilize magnetic properties to maintain output voltage, offering stability and immunity to fluctuations. They are known for their robustness and less maintenance. In contrast, tap switching regulators adjust voltage levels using mechanical taps on a transformer, providing precise voltage control. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring consistent voltage supply in electrical distribution systems, addressing different application needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
