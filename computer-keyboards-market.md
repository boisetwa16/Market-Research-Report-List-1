<p><p>The computer keyboard market is divided into two main types: desktop PC keyboards and notebook keyboards. Desktop PC keyboards are designed for use with stationary computers, offering larger layouts, mechanical switches, and enhanced ergonomic features for prolonged use. In contrast, notebook keyboards are compact, integrated into laptops, and often utilize membrane switches to save space and maintain a slim profile. Both markets cater to different user needs, with desktop keyboards focusing on performance and comfort, while notebook keyboards emphasize portability and convenience.</p></p>
