<p><p>The manual cutting equipment market includes various technologies used for metal cutting and shaping. Plasma cutting utilizes a high-temperature plasma arc to cut through conductive materials. Oxy-fuel cutting employs a flame fueled by oxygen and gas for cutting metals. Laser cutting uses focused light beams for precision cuts. Waterjet cutting uses high-pressure water mixed with abrasives for versatile cutting without thermal distortion. Carbon arc cutting employs an electric arc to melt metals, primarily for repairs or heavy fabrication. Each method caters to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
