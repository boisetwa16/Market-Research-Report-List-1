<p><p>The laser glass marking market comprises various technologies, primarily CO2 laser marking and UV laser marking. CO2 laser marking utilizes a longer wavelength that effectively engraves and etches glass, creating high-contrast designs suitable for various applications. UV laser marking, on the other hand, employs shorter wavelengths, allowing for precise and fine detailing without causing thermal damage, making it ideal for intricate patterns and high-resolution markings. Both technologies cater to different industrial needs, from branding to personalization.</p></p>
