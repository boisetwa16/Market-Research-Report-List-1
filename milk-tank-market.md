<p><p>The milk tank market consists of various types designed for efficient storage and handling of milk. Horizontal closed tanks are typically used for large-scale storage, featuring a wide base for stability. Silos are vertical structures that maximize storage capacity while minimizing land use. Vertical closed tanks provide sealed environments for preserving milk quality, beneficial for smaller operations. Open tanks, while less common, are used for immediate processing needs, facilitating easy access but requiring more frequent cleaning and monitoring.</p></p>
