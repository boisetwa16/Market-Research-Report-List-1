<p><ul><li>Backbone Liquid Crystal Polyester</li><li>Side Chain Liquid Crystal Polyester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Crystal Polyester (LCP) market is primarily divided into two types: Backbone Liquid Crystal Polyester and Side Chain Liquid Crystal Polyester. Backbone LCP features a rigid molecular structure that provides excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, Side Chain LCP contains flexible segments that enhance processability and reduce melt viscosity, allowing for easier shaping and molding. Both types find applications in electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors due to their unique characteristics.</p></p>
