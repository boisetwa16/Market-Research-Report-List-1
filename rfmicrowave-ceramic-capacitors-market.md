<p><p>The RF/Microwave Ceramic Capacitors market is categorized into Class 1 and Class 2 ceramic capacitors. Class 1 capacitors offer stable capacitance over varying temperatures and voltages, making them ideal for precision applications. In contrast, Class 2 capacitors provide higher capacitance values and are more temperature-sensitive, suited for general-purpose applications where size and cost are critical. Both types serve essential roles in filtering, bypassing, and decoupling in RF and microwave circuits, impacting performance and efficiency.</p></p>
