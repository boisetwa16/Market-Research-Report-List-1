Anti-strip agents improve adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, preventing moisture damage. Adhesion promoters further enhance bond strength, contributing to durability. Other additives may include antioxidants and colorants, each tailored to specific conditions and applications, ultimately improving the quality of asphalt mixtures.</p></p>
