<p><p>The metal fibers market includes several production techniques. Vacuum deposition involves applying thin layers of metal onto surfaces in a controlled environment. Coating metal powder with binders combines metal particles with adhesives to enhance strength and durability. Sputter coating entails ejecting metal atoms onto a substrate, creating a thin film. Electroless coating, a chemical deposition process, enables uniform metal layers without electricity. Each method serves distinct applications, balancing cost, efficiency, and specific material properties for various industries.</p></p>
