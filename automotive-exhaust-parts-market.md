<p><p>The automotive exhaust parts market includes components like mufflers, pipes, catalytic converters, and others. Mufflers reduce noise by dampening exhaust gases, ensuring quieter vehicle operation. Pipes channel exhaust gases from the engine to the tailpipe, maintaining proper flow and pressure. Catalytic converters reduce harmful emissions by converting pollutants into less toxic substances. Other components include sensors, resonators, and heat shields, which enhance performance, durability, and compliance with emission standards. Together, these parts ensure efficient, eco-friendly, and noise-controlled vehicle operation.</p></p>
