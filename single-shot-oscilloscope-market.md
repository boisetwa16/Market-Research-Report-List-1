<p><strong>The Single-shot Oscilloscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Storage Oscilloscope</li><li>Phosphor Oscilloscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-shot oscilloscope market comprises primarily two types: storage oscilloscopes and phosphor oscilloscopes. Storage oscilloscopes capture and store waveforms for detailed analysis, enabling the observation of transient signals. They utilize digital memory to retain data, providing enhanced viewing capabilities. In contrast, phosphor oscilloscopes use a phosphorescent screen to display waveforms, depicting signal changes in real-time. Their ability to visualize fast events makes them valuable in various applications, including electronics testing and signal processing. Both types serve distinct roles in waveform analysis and diagnostics.</p></p>
