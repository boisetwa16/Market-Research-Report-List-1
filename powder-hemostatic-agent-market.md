<p><p>The powder hemostatic agent market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable hemostatic agents. Absorbable hemostatic agents are designed to be integrated and gradually broken down by the body, facilitating natural healing processes while managing bleeding. In contrast, non-absorbable hemostatic agents remain in the body, providing long-lasting control of hemorrhage and often require surgical removal. Both types serve critical roles in surgical and trauma settings by promoting clot formation and reducing bleeding complications efficiently.</p></p>
