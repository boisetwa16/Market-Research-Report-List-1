<p><strong>The Architectural Tempered Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tempered Glass</li><li>Bent Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The architectural tempered glass market is primarily categorized into flat tempered glass and bent tempered glass. Flat tempered glass is produced through a process that strengthens flat sheets for applications like facades, windows, and doors, ensuring durability and safety. Bent tempered glass, on the other hand, is shaped through heating and bending, making it suitable for curved architectural designs, skylights, and partitions. Both types enhance aesthetic appeal while providing impact resistance and thermal stability in modern constructions.</p></p>
