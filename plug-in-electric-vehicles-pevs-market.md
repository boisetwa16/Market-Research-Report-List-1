<p><p>Plug-in Electric Vehicles (PEVs) include Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), Plug-In Hybrid Vehicles (PHEVs), and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). PHEVs combine an electric motor with an internal combustion engine, offering both electric-only and hybrid driving modes. HEVs use a combination of an electric motor and a gasoline engine but cannot be plugged in, relying on regenerative braking and the engine to charge the battery. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing efficiency, range, and environmental impact.</p></p>
