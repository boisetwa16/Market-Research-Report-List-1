<p><p>The radiation measuring instrument market consists of various types, each designed for specific applications. Scintillation detectors use scintillating materials to detect and measure ionizing radiation, offering high sensitivity. Neutron detectors specialize in identifying neutrons emitted from radioactive sources, commonly used in nuclear facilities. Geiger counters are portable devices that detect and quantify ionizing radiation, suitable for general safety and environmental monitoring. Other instruments may include dosimeters and ionization chambers, catering to specialized needs in research, healthcare, and safety compliance.</p></p>
