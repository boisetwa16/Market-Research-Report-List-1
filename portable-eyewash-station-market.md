<p><p>The Portable Eyewash Station market consists primarily of two types: Ordinary Type and Pressure Type Eyewash Stations. Ordinary Type Eyewash Stations are typically gravity-fed and require minimal setup, providing immediate access to flushing fluid in emergencies. In contrast, Pressure Type Eyewash Stations are pressurized to ensure a higher flow rate, delivering a more effective wash for hazardous substances. Both types are essential in workplaces where chemical exposure risks exist, ensuring rapid response to eye and face injuries.</p></p>
